Effects of COVID-19 on MBBS in Abroad

The COVID-19 pandemic has already or is likely to, affect the higher education sector in many ways. Coronavirus stormed through the global economy grinding activities almost to a halt. This applies to the normal operations of admissions in many countries. With the uncertainty caused on account of the COVID-19 pandemic, students who are planning to study MBBS Abroad would be concerned about their safety and their chances of getting into the University of their choice. We would like to assure the students that the extremely bright future awaits them after this COVID-19 pandemic is controlled and eradicated in due time.
